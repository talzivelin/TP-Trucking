Trucking Services

TP Trucking, a common carrier and licensed property broker, specializes in moving a wide range of goods and materials in all 48 contiguous states and Canada. With a winning combination of advanced equipment, professional expertise, in-house logistics management and a commitment to safety, TP Trucking delivers.

The Best Equipment

By investing in the newest advanced trucks, trailers and technical equipment−including flatbeds, curtain vans, roll tops and dry vans−TP Trucking has what it takes to move goods anywhere in North America.

The Best Team

We have a proud history of hiring and retaining experienced, professional drivers, dispatchers and support staff who treat each load with the careful attention it deserves. Apply to drive for us ››

The Best Management

Our in-house logistics department ensures efficient dispatching, handling and routing of part or full loads−and with terminals on both coasts, we are perfectly positioned to provide best-in-class, cost-effective transportation solutions.

A Commitment to Safety

With one of the best safety performances on record, TP Trucking has won dozens of awards over the past 15 years. Our commitment to safety is also reflected in our Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration records.
